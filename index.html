Creamy Chicken Pasta

Description

This creamy chicken pasta is rich, delicious and easy to prepare. perfect for lunch or dinner.

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 2-3people
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pasta
  • 1 cup of cooked chicken(shredded)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper, Add salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the pastta until soft, then drain and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and add shredded chicken.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 3 minutes.
  4. Add cheese, salt and black pepper.
  5. Mixed in the cooked pasta and stir it until well coated in the sauce.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!.

Tips

For Extra flavour add garlic or chilli flakes. you can also use parmesan cheese for a stronger taste!

Nutrition Facts (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 18g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbs: 38g
